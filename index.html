About us

VoidWatch is a student-led astronomy club dedicated to exploring the wonders of the universe. Our aim is to make space science fun, accessible, and exciting for everyone‚Äîwhether you're a beginner or already passionate about the stars. We conduct stargazing nights, space talks, and interactive sessions to spark curiosity about the cosmos. From planets and constellations to black holes and galaxies, we cover a wide range of topics. Join VoidWatch and begin your journey into the mysteries of the night sky.

Who can join this club

VoidWatch is open to all students who are interested in exploring the mysteries of space and the universe. Whether you're a complete beginner or already passionate about astronomy, you are welcome here. You don't need any prior knowledge ‚Äî just curiosity and a love for the stars!

Registration process:
